Mental Health

Mental health refers to a person's emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It encompasses various aspects of life, including how individuals think, feel, and act. Mental health affects one's ability to handle stress, relate to others, make decisions, and navigate the challenges of daily life

Emotional Well-being:

The ability to understand and manage one's emotions, cope with stress, and experience positive feelings.

Psychological Well-being:

Having a sense of purpose, self-acceptance, and personal growth. It involves developing resilience and adapting to life's changes.

Social Well-being:

Building and maintaining healthy relationships, being socially connected, and having a support system.

Promoting mental health involves creating supportive environments, reducing stigma, and ensuring access to appropriate mental health services. Taking care of one's mental health is as important as taking care of physical health, and individuals are encouraged to prioritize both aspects for a holistic and balanced lifestyle.
